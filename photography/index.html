About

Over the past few years, I've enjoyed taking pictures on a handful of different cameras: my Sony a5000, Canon AE-1, Canon TLb, and iPhone SE. When shooting film, I generally use Kodak Colorplus 200 or Kentmere Pan 400. I typically develop the black and white Kentmere film myself and occasionally make prints in the darkroom in addition to scanning my negatives. I've had a handful of images published in Rowan University's Avant Magazine over the past few years and I always enjoy seeing my images come to life on paper. Interested in purchasing full resolution files or prints? Just send me an email for pricing.
